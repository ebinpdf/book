About.

Thomas Vazhakunnathu

I am a Spiritual Scientist, Teacher & Author from India. My conscious spiritual journey started at the age of 23 in the year 1994 when I had a profound inner transformation that changed the course of my life. Since then I have devoted my life towards seeking and living the ultimate truth of life. By the year 2004, I was able to understand that there is no religion or path that can claim exclusivity of truth or salvation. Further, I understood the oneness of everything, and that there is ‘truth’ in everything, albeit with varying importance, and knowing these truths, particularly the fundamental truths, can free us from many limitations and sufferings and lead to peace and happiness in ascending order and ultimately to a blissful existence.

As a conscious observer of life, I enjoy spending most of my time investigating, understanding, integrating, and living the fundamental truths of life. You won’t find much information about me on the internet, as I have limited social media interactions. The first draft of my book containing the spiritual theory of life was ready in 2013 and briefly published, but was withdrawn soon as I wanted some more clarity on certain concepts. Have continued to meditate on those concepts and when I got more clarity rewrote the book during 2019-20 and published in November 2020.

My ultimate objective for the present life is to awaken and transform as many as possible and bring peace, health, and harmony to planet Earth. I am open to working with everyone who shares this objective. I places more emphasis on living the truth or walking the talk than anything else as that is the only way one can move up in the scale of life. For me, there is nothing more important than living a blissful life with ever-expanding consciousness. My formal education includes a masters in English Literature and in Business Administration.

The purpose of this page is to make myself available on the web and share fundamental concepts explained in the book and also new insights I get, through posts and blogs.
